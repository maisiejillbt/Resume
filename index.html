Forage the
Bay

View on Github

Find what you can forage and where with Forage the Bay, a data visualization tool for foragers of all experience levels.

With Forage the Bay you can easily find a food to forage in your area no matter the season! Forage the Bay is made up of data from my personal knowledge of the bay area as well as information from California State Parks and Forests.





The main map to the left the page can be easily interacted with. You can select your current season, and the area you want to visit, this brings up information and regulations for your selected park as well as the available foods during that season.

For more information and identification, I have included links to each foods wikipedia page.



Zodiac Map

View on Github

With Zodiac Map you can easily toggle between zodiac signs to view the constellations as if you're right there with them in space.

Zodiac Map uses ThreeJs in order to map the stars and make them appear on your screen in 3D. It also uses data from AstroNexus in order to map the stars as they would be found in space.





Click a zodiac sign to view that constellation as you would from earth, of interact with the map by clicking 'explore' use scroll to zoom in and out or drag with your mouse to change your position.



Twinterest

View on Github

Twinterest is a fullstack clone based on Pinterest.

Pinterest is an image-based social media network where users and businesses can promote and explore their interests by pinning images and videos on virtual bulletin boards. With Twinterest users are able to follow eachother, add pins to their boards, create and edit new pins and boards, and view all other users pins.





One of my main goals for Twinterest was to stick to the design of Pinterest as closely as possible. Making sure to measure and adjust my cloned site as needed. For me the appearence of the page is just as important as the functionality.
